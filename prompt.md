When the stopwatch countdown reaches zero, the following should happen simultaneously:

An overdue timer starts counting upward (e.g., +00:01, +00:02...) to show elapsed overdue time.
A ring/alarm sound plays (looping or once).
A rectangular popup appears as a small companion window, containing:

A label like "Overdue" or "Time's Up"
The live overdue timer count
A Close button that stops the sound and dismisses the popup



The popup should stay on top and be non-intrusive in size.
